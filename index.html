About the Band

With roots tracing back to the underbelly of the Burlington, VT college scene, Bummer Status is comprised of four like-minded musicians with a passion for writing and performing incredibly tasteful jams.

The band members are guitarist/vocalist Nicholas Andrews, bassist Elliott Kenney, drummer/vocalist Doug Lane, and pianist/vocalist Mike McCarthy.
